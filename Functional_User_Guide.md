#  CHMS: Functional User Guide

This document outlines all the user-facing features of the  Hotel Management System. It is designed to provide a clear understanding of the application's capabilities from an operational perspective.

---

## 1. Management Dashboard
The "Control Center" of the hotel.
- **Real-Time Occupancy Grid**: A visual map of all rooms across all floors. Statuses are color-coded (Available, Occupied, Cleaning).
- **Performance Metrics**: Live tracking of today's Revenue (RWF) and current Occupancy Rate percentage.
- **Quick Actions**: One-click access to "New Reservation" and "Room Details" via hover-over interactions.

## 2. Reservations & Guest Profiles
Managing the lifecycle of a guest's stay.
- **Specific Room Assignment**: Assign guests to exact room numbers during booking.
- **Biometric & Contact Matrix**: Capture comprehensive guest data including Full Name, Phone, Email, Nationality, and ID/Passport numbers.
- **Automated Rate Calculation**: The system automatically calculates total stay costs based on room type and date ranges.
- **Temporal Tracking**: Manage Check-in and Check-out cycles with precision.

## 3. Financials & Folio Settlement
Professional billing and checkout.
- **Digital Guest Folio**: An ongoing account for every guest that tracks all room costs and service charges.
- **Itemized Invoicing**: Generate clear invoices that separate room costs from ancillary services.
- **One-Click PDF Generation**: Print professional, brand-ready invoices for guests at checkout.
- **Checkout Workflow**: Closing a folio automatically triggers a "Cleaning" status for the room to alert housekeeping.

## 4. Restaurant & Bar (POS)
Streamlined sales for the hotel’s dining facilities.
- **Categorized Menu**: Browse items by category (Breakfast, Drinks, Main Course, etc.) or search by keyword.
- **Flexible Billing**:
    - **Resident Charging**: Link a meal or drink directly to a guest's room number for a single final bill.
    - **Walk-in Billing**: Generate standalone invoices for non-resident guests.
- **Interactive Cart**: Build complex orders with multiple items and live price totals.

## 5. Event & Conference Operations
Managing high-value venue spaces.
- **Venue Gallery**: View all available halls and meeting rooms with their capacity and hourly rates.
- **Master Event Schedule**: A chronological timeline view of all booked events to prevent scheduling conflicts.
- **Event Registration**: Dedicated workflow for booking venues for weddings, conferences, or corporate meetings.

## 6. Inventory & Supply Chain
Keeping the hotel stocked and running.
- **Stock Tracking**: Monitor levels of essential supplies (toiletries, kitchen ingredients, linens).
- **Low-Stock Alerts**: Visual indicators when items drop below a predefined "Minimum Stock" level.
- **Category Management**: Organize inventory by type (e.g., Housekeeping, Kitchen, Bar).

## 7. Web Inquiries (CRM)
Managing potential leads and customer questions.
- **Centralized Inquiry Inbox**: View all messages sent via the hotel’s public website.
- **Status Tracking**: Mark inquiries as "Read" or "Replied" to ensure no guest is ignored.
- **Lead Context**: See guest preferences (room type, expected dates) directly in the inquiry.

## 8. Business Intelligence (Reports)
Data-driven decision making.
- **Financial Summaries**: View revenue breakdowns over custom time periods.
- **Operational Analytics**: Track occupancy trends and popular menu items.
- **Excel Export**: Download all financial and guest data into `.xlsx` format for external accounting or analysis.
